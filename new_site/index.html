Our Mission

We’re on a mission to utilize the transformative power of technology to elevate non-profits and small businesses on their mission. We believe as everyone should, “Giving is receiving.”

Our Story

When Siddhant Khapra first started building websites through his passion in the summer of 2020, he saw the immense power of technology to spread messages and ideas through the world and first-handily saw how non-profits and small-businesses were struggling to survive. He immediately came up with the concept of digitalizing non-profits and small businesses and started Onex Technology.
